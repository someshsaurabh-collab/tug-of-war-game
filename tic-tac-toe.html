🕹️ How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Player 1 is ✕ and Player 2 is ○. Take turns clicking empty cells. Get 3 in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win! If all cells are filled with no winner, it's a draw. Best of 5 rounds wins!
