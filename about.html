🚀 Our Story

BlitzGames started with a simple idea — what if you could just open your browser and start playing a great game in seconds? No App Store, no download bar, no loading screens. Just instant fun.

We built our first game, Tug of War, as a fun 2-player dice battle you could play with a friend on the same phone or across the internet. The response was amazing — people wanted more.

So we kept going. Snake. Tic Tac Toe. Memory Match. Each game hand-crafted to be smooth, mobile-friendly, and genuinely enjoyable. And we're just getting started — new games are added regularly as we grow the collection.

🎯 Our Mission

Games should be accessible to everyone. Not just people with powerful PCs or expensive phones. Not just people willing to hand over their email address or credit card. Everyone.

That's why every game on BlitzGames is completely free, works in any modern browser, and requires zero setup. We believe the best gaming moments happen spontaneously — when you share a link with a friend and you're both playing within 5 seconds.
